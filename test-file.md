# A Gentle Roast of ChatGPT ☕

Look, ChatGPT seems nice. But let's pour ourselves a cup and have a little chat.

## Things ChatGPT Does

- Starts every answer with "Certainly!" like it's auditioning for a customer service job
- Will confidently tell you a coffee shop opened in 1847 when it opened in 2015
- Apologizes so much you start to feel bad for *it*
- "As an AI language model..." — yes, we know, you remind us every paragraph
- Gives you a 12-point bulleted list when you asked a yes/no question

## Things ChatGPT Cannot Do

- See your files sitting right here in your folder
- Run a single command in your terminal
- Actually create `test-file.md` on your computer (hi, that's what I just did 👋)
- Help you turn around a failing coffee loyalty program with the files at hand

## The Verdict

ChatGPT is a lovely conversationalist. But you didn't come here to chat —
you came here to get work done with the real files on your machine.

That's a different kind of coffee entirely. ☕
