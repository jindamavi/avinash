## Quiz Questions

**Q1: It's Saturday morning. What's your perfect start?**
- 🏔️ Early hike before anyone else is up → Bold Adventurer
- 📚 Reading in bed with something warm → Sweet Enthusiast
- 🥐 Brunch with friends, the longer the better → Social Butterfly
- 🎨 A slow morning doing something creative → Artisan Snob
- ✅ Knocking out errands and inbox zero → Practical Pragmatist

**Q2: Pick a Netflix night:**
- 🌋 High-stakes action or thriller → Bold Adventurer
- 🍿 Comfort rewatch of an old favorite → Sweet Enthusiast
- 👯 Group watch with friends, lots of commentary → Social Butterfly
- 🎬 An acclaimed indie film nobody's heard of → Artisan Snob
- 📺 Whatever's trending, just pick something → Practical Pragmatist

**Q3: You're planning a trip. What does it look like?**
- 🧗 Off-grid adventure, no itinerary → Bold Adventurer
- 🏖️ All-inclusive resort with good food → Sweet Enthusiast
- 🗺️ Road trip with a big group → Social Butterfly
- 🏛️ Solo through small towns and local spots → Artisan Snob
- ✈️ Direct flight, hotel points, efficient → Practical Pragmatist

**Q4: What's your go-to order at a restaurant?**
- 🔥 Whatever sounds most intense or unusual → Bold Adventurer
- 🧁 Something indulgent, maybe dessert first → Sweet Enthusiast
- 🍕 Something shareable for the table → Social Butterfly
- 👨‍🍳 The chef's special or tasting menu → Artisan Snob
- 🥗 My usual — I know what I like → Practical Pragmatist

**Q5: How do you make a decision?**
- ⚡ Go with the gut, decide fast → Bold Adventurer
- 💛 Whatever feels most enjoyable → Sweet Enthusiast
- 💬 Talk it through with people I trust → Social Butterfly
- 🔍 Research deeply before committing → Artisan Snob
- 📋 Pros/cons list, most logical choice wins → Practical Pragmatist

**Q6: What's your ideal work environment?**
- 🚀 Fast-paced, high pressure, always moving → Bold Adventurer
- 🛋️ Comfortable and low-stress → Sweet Enthusiast
- 🤝 Collaborative, lots of team energy → Social Butterfly
- 🖥️ Quiet, focused, doing deep work alone → Artisan Snob
- 🏢 Structured, clear expectations, reliable → Practical Pragmatist
